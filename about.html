Our Story

How We Got Here

Nature Dispatch started with a straightforward observation: too many shippers in the Columbus area were getting lost in the shuffle of mega-carriers and faceless brokerages. Calls went to voicemail. Updates came late — or never. Freight sat at docks waiting for trucks that were "on the way."

We saw a gap and filled it. We built a small, focused carrier operation based in Columbus with one goal: be the carrier that actually picks up the phone. Be the carrier that shows up when we say we will. Be the carrier that treats every shipper like they're our only customer.

Today, we run 11 company-owned dry vans across the Midwest, Southeast, and Northeast. We're not the biggest carrier on the road — and we don't want to be. We want to be the best carrier for the shippers who choose us.
