Requirements

What We're Looking For

We don't need a perfect driving history or 20 years of experience. We need drivers who are reliable, professional, and take pride in their work.

  • ✓ Valid CDL-A license
  • ✓ Minimum 1 year OTR or regional experience (preferred)
  • ✓ Clean MVR — no DUI/DWI in the last 5 years
  • ✓ Must pass DOT physical and drug screening
  • ✓ Able to operate in Columbus, OH area
  • ✓ Professional attitude and communication skills
