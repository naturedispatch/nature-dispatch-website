Our Approach

Safety Is How We Operate

From hiring to delivery, safety is embedded in every decision we make.

👤

Driver Screening

Every driver goes through background checks, MVR review, drug screening, and DOT physical verification before they ever sit behind the wheel of our equipment.

📖

Ongoing Training

Drivers receive regular safety briefings, defensive driving refreshers, and updates on HOS regulation changes. Safety training never stops.

📡

ELD Monitoring

Samsara ELDs track hours of service in real time. Our dispatch team monitors compliance continuously — no driver ever runs over hours.

🔎

Pre-Trip Inspections

Mandatory pre-trip and post-trip inspections on every run. Documented, photographed, and reviewed by operations before dispatch.

🚬

Zero Tolerance Policy

Zero tolerance for substance abuse, distracted driving, and unsafe behavior. Violations result in immediate removal from service — no exceptions.

⚙

Equipment Maintenance

Preventive maintenance schedules, regular brake inspections, tire monitoring, and immediate repair protocols keep our fleet road-ready at all times.
